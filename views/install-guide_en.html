4 steps to activate your custom lock chime

Works with Model 3, Model Y and any Tesla equipped with the outside speaker. Follow along and replace the factory chime in minutes.

No disassembly · Plug & play
Swap sounds anytime
What you need
  • tesla_sounds.zip downloaded and unzipped on phone / laptop
  • Vehicle firmware ≥ 2021.32 with Boombox / Custom Lock Sound available
  • USB drive or SD card (FAT32 / exFAT, ≥ 200MB free)
  • External speaker enabled and not muted

Step-by-step walkthrough

1
Copy the sound pack to your drive

Place the extracted tesla_sounds (recommended folder name: Boombox) in the root directory. Keep the folder structure unchanged.

Tip: backup Dashcam / Sentry files before reusing an existing drive.
2
Insert the USB into the vehicle

Use the glovebox or center console USB port. Wait for the USB icon to appear at the top of the screen.

Not detected? Reseat the drive or format via Safety & Security > Format USB.
3
Open Boombox / Exterior sound menu

Navigate to Controls > Entertainment > Boombox (or Sound > External speaker). Choose “Custom” and browse the USB drive.

Ensure “Enable External Speaker” is toggled on if presented.
4
Preview & save

Click to audition a sound, then confirm to apply. Lock the car once to verify the new chime.

Adjust volume in the menu or choose another preset if volume is low.
FAQ
Boombox isn’t showing up
Check your vehicle year, firmware version, and whether an external speaker is installed. Older models may not support the feature.
No sound or stuttering
Use a fast USB 3.0 drive, ensure the file format is MP3/WAV, and reboot the infotainment if needed.
Can I control volume?
Many builds expose volume in the external speaker settings. We also provide multiple gain levels in the pack.
Pro tips
  • Use driver Profiles to store different sound selections for each family member.
  • Combine with Sentry Mode audio for themed experiences.
  • Download future updates from our emails, overwrite the USB folder, and you’re done.
  • Need to revert? Simply choose “Default” in the menu.
